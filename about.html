Our Story & Sacred Mission

Who We Are

Radheratan
Agarbatti

Radheratan Agarbatti is a proudly Indian brand under the umbrella of LC Enterprises — born from a deep reverence for spiritual tradition and a lifelong commitment to purity in every sacred offering.

We craft incense sticks, dhoopbatti, and puja essentials carrying the essence of ancient Indian fragrance wisdom — made with natural botanicals, resins, and pure essential oils — for the modern devotee who refuses to compromise on quality.

From individual households to grand temple complexes, Radheratan serves as the fragrant bridge between the earthly and the divine. Every stick we make is an act of devotion in itself.
